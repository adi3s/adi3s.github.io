Darjeeling

Darjeeling, located in the state of West Bengal, India, is famous for its lush tea gardens. The city offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural diversity, making it a mesmerizing destination for travelers.

Why I Love Darjeeling

The serene atmosphere, cool climates, and exquisite landscapes make this destination a perfect getaway.

The charm of the colonial-era architecture, vibrant local markets, and warm hospitality adds to its beauty.

My Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

  1. Arrive at Darjeeling
  2. Local Breakfast, including a cup of the famous Darjeeling tea
  3. Explore Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park, home to unique species like Red Panda and Snow Leopard
  4. Stroll Mall Road for shopping.

Day 2: Tea Gardens and Culture

  1. Sunrise at Tiger Hills
  2. Ghoom Monastery for local Buddhist culture
  3. Tour of Happy Valley Tea Estate to learn about tea production and enjoy a tasting session
  4. Japanese Pagoda for panoramic views of the city

Day 3: Departure

  1. Ride Darjeeling Railway, also known as Toy Train
  2. Lloyd Botanical gardens to see a collection of Himalayan plants and orchids
  3. Depart from Darjeeling
